7. Results of this study revealed that explainable AI significantly enhances user trust in AI systems.  
    本研究結果顯示，可解釋人工智慧顯著增強了使用者對人工智慧系統的信任。
    
8. The results obtained confirm that AI-driven automation can lead to increased efficiency and productivity.  
    所得結果證實，人工智慧驅動的自動化可以提高效率和生產力。
    
9. Our analysis of the data gives reasonable results and provides insights into the relationship between AI transparency and perceived fairness.  
    我們的數據分析得出了合理的結果，並提供了關於人工智慧透明性與感知公平性之間關係的見解。
    
10. Our model simulation results show that engaging in immersive virtual reality games, such as the snowball-throwing game, can effectively distract from discomfort associated with headset use.  
    我們的模型模擬結果顯示，參與沉浸式虛擬實境遊戲，例如丟雪球遊戲，能有效分散使用者對於佩戴頭戴裝置不適感的注意力。
    
11. The main findings reveal that generative AI tools are perceived as highly useful and engaging by users, even if some ethical considerations need further attention.  
    主要研究結果顯示，使用者認為生成式人工智慧工具非常有用且具吸引力，即使某些倫理考量仍需進一步關注。
    
12. This interview and survey findings indicate that while users are optimistic about the benefits of AI, there are significant concerns regarding its societal impacts and the need for greater transparency.  
    本次訪談與問卷調查結果指出，儘管使用者對人工智慧的益處持樂觀態度，但對於其社會影響以及對更高透明度的需求仍存在顯著擔憂。
